Get the extension

Yukti isn't on the Chrome Web Store yet — early access installs in under a minute.

  1. Download: Download Yukti (zip)  and unzip it.
  2. Open chrome://extensions, switch on Developer mode (top-right).
  3. Click Load unpacked → select the extension folder from the unzipped download.
